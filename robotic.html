About Robotic exhibition

An Estonian-based organization called Robotex organizes Robotex International, the annual robotics festival, which brings together thousands of engineers, executives, students, and families to hear from the leaders of the technology industry, explore new start-ups, build robots and explore the newest technology.

About Robotic exhibition

An Estonian-based organization called Robotex organizes Robotex International, the annual robotics festival, which brings together thousands of engineers, executives, students, and families to hear from the leaders of the technology industry, explore new start-ups, build robots and explore the newest technology.

Property of Event

  • International robotics competitions with the aim of developing students' intelligence
  • In 2008, the event moved to the premises of Taltech Sports Hall, where larger area and better conditions were ensured. In addition to competitions, various expositions, exhibitions, and workshops for young people took place.
  • Students between the ages of 9 and 17, boys and girls, are eligible to participate in the competition
